Thousands of community members are returning home from federal prisons under the First Step Act. Here are some valuable resources you can access to help with a successful transition.

Lyft ride credits

Apply for free ride share credits to help get you around.

 
 

Mobile phones

Apply for a free phone with up to three months of free service .

 
 

Online courses

Access free online courses focused on personal and professional development.

 
 

therapy & Counseling

Chat with a professional from the comfort of home or in a location near you.

 
 

job Preparedness

Get ready for job interviews with professional assistance.

 

Personal & Professional Development

Personalized coaching to help you develop and improve confidence, speaking skills, and more.
